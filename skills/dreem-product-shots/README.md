# dreem-product-shots

Turn any product photo into clean, store-ready Product Shots - white background for marketplaces, styled scenes for your PDP, any aspect ratio, up to 4K.

## Try it

> "Make this a white-background square shot for Amazon." (attach a photo)

> "Give me PDP shots of these 6 products on a warm linen backdrop - here's the CSV with image links."

## How it protects your credits

You always see the full generation plan (what, how many, which formats) and approve it before anything runs. Every batch ends with a QA pass that flags logo/pattern issues for a quick fix in Dreem Studio's editor.

## Needs

The Dreem connector + a product photo (file upload or public image URL).
