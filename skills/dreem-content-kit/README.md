# dreem-content-kit

One product image in, a launch-ready set out: marketplace Product Shot, PDP on-model shots, social formats, and a short video. Planned as one kit, one cost preview, one approval.

## Try it

> "New product launching Friday - here's the photo. I want the full set: PDP, Amazon, Instagram, and a short video."

> "Run the content kit on the top 3 products from the audit."

## How it runs

You pick the model and styles once, approve one plan with the total cost, and the kit generates in a smart order - Product Shot first as a cheap quality check, video last, chained from the best generated still. One manifest, honest QA flags on every asset.

## Needs

The Dreem connector + one product photo (front and back if you have both).
