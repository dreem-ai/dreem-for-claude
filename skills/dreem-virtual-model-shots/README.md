# dreem-virtual-model-shots

Put any garment on any of 400+ virtual talents. Flat lay in, on-model out - across body types, ages, sizes, and ethnicities.

## Try it

> "Put this dress on a model in her 40s, mid-size, natural studio look." (attach a flat lay)

> "Show this jacket on 5 models across the size range for our PDP."

> "Replace the model in this photo - we lost the license. Keep the garment exactly."

## Why it works

Dreem keeps your actual garment - logo, stitching, fit - and changes who wears it. Every batch ends with a fidelity QA pass (logos, patterns, color), and you approve the full plan and cost before anything generates.

## Needs

The Dreem connector + garment photo(s). Front and back shots make back poses accurate.
