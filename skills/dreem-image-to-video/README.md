# dreem-image-to-video

Turn a product still into a 5-10 second video. Subtle motion for your PDP, scroll-stopping movement for TikTok, Reels, and Stories.

## Try it

> "Make a 5 second video from this shot for Reels - subtle camera movement."

> "Turn our best-seller's PDP image into a slow-turn video."

## Good to know

Works best from a clean still - and it chains: generate an on-model shot with `dreem-virtual-model-shots`, then animate it. You approve the plan and cost first; video spends more credits than stills.

## Needs

The Dreem connector + one still image (upload or URL).
